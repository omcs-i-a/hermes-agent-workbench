# Research Report Template

Use this template for AI Agent research reports.

```markdown
# [Report Title]

Date: YYYY-MM-DD
Author/Agent: Research Agent
Status: draft | reviewed | final

## 1. Research Question

State the main question in one or two sentences.

## 2. Executive Summary

Short answer in 3-6 bullets.

- Finding 1.
- Finding 2.
- Finding 3.

## 3. Scope

Included:

- ...

Excluded:

- ...

Assumptions:

- ...

## 4. Key Findings

### Finding 1: [Name]

Summary:

Evidence:

- Source: [Title](URL), date/version if known.
- Relevant claim or observation.

Confidence: High | Medium | Low

### Finding 2: [Name]

Summary:

Evidence:

- Source: [Title](URL), date/version if known.

Confidence: High | Medium | Low

## 5. Source Table

| # | Source | Type | Date/Version | Why it matters | Reliability |
|---|--------|------|--------------|----------------|-------------|
| 1 | [Title](URL) | paper/docs/repo/blog/benchmark | YYYY-MM-DD or unknown | ... | High/Medium/Low |
| 2 | [Title](URL) | ... | ... | ... | ... |

## 6. Analysis

Synthesize the evidence by theme.

Suggested subsections:

- Background and context.
- Taxonomy or categories.
- Comparison of approaches.
- Trade-offs.
- Implementation implications.
- Evaluation implications.

## 7. Comparison Table

Use when comparing tools, papers, methods, or agents.

| Item | Strengths | Weaknesses | Best Use | Evidence |
|------|-----------|------------|----------|----------|
| A | ... | ... | ... | [source](URL) |
| B | ... | ... | ... | [source](URL) |

## 8. Open Questions

- Question 1.
- Question 2.
- Question 3.

## 9. Risks and Limitations

- Limited source coverage.
- Outdated sources.
- Vendor bias.
- Lack of reproducible benchmarks.
- Conflicting evidence.

## 10. Confidence Assessment

Overall confidence: High | Medium | Low

Rationale:

- Why this confidence level was chosen.
- Which claims are strongest.
- Which claims need more evidence.

## 11. Recommended Next Actions

- Action 1.
- Action 2.
- Action 3.

## 12. Appendix

Additional notes, raw observations, search queries, or discarded sources.
```
